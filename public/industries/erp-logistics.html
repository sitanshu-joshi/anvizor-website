Streamlining Enterprise Operations and Logistics with Intelligent, Connected Systems

At Anvizor Solutions, we engineer smart ERP and logistics solutions that optimize supply chains, drive operational agility, and provide real-time business visibility. With deep domain expertise and close client collaboration, we’ve successfully built ERP integration hubs, streamlined petroleum logistics, and introduced AI-powered forecasting tools tailored to our partners’ evolving needs.

Core ERP & Logistics Capabilities

  • Iteration Hub Architecture: Unified platforms to manage full order lifecycles—Product, Purchase Order, Sales Order, Transfer Order, Goods Receipt, Invoice, and Voucher—across source and target ERP systems.
  • ERP Integration Models: Supporting one-to-many, many-to-many, and many-to-one data synchronization to ensure smooth communication across systems.
  • Smart Order Management Systems: Digitized and automated systems to handle orders, approvals, tracking, and fulfillment in real time.

Logistics Intelligence in Action

  • Petroleum Logistics Optimization: Custom-built solutions to manage and monitor petroleum order chains, tanker routing, and real-time dispatch.
  • Fleet Tracking & Geo-Fencing: Integration with GPS and mapping APIs for visibility, compliance, and performance analysis.
  • Warehouse & Inventory Automation: Smart dashboards and rules-based engines to reduce bottlenecks and track goods with precision.

AI-Powered Predictive Tools

  • Demand Forecasting: Machine learning algorithms to predict material and inventory needs.
  • Delivery Time Estimation: AI-driven delivery ETAs based on historical patterns, traffic, and order density.
  • Exception Alerts & Recommendations: Proactive alerts to mitigate delays, loss, and supply chain inefficiencies.

Why Choose Anvizor for ERP & Logistics Solutions?

  • Tight Domain Expertise: From ERP lifecycle workflows to petroleum supply chains, we’ve seen it and solved it.
  • System Interoperability: Smooth integration with major ERP platforms including SAP, Oracle, and custom systems.
  • Engineering Excellence: Stable, scalable, and extensible systems tailored to your business.
  • Decision-Centric Dashboards: Actionable intelligence and visibility at your fingertips.
