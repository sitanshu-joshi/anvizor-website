Revolutionising Healthcare Through Intelligent, Integrated, and Human-Centered Technology

At Anvizor Solutions, we are redefining the future of healthcare through precision-engineered software solutions that prioritize patient care, provider efficiency, and medical innovation. With 15+ years of domain expertise, our engineering teams have delivered enterprise-grade health tech platforms for hospitals, research institutions, and diagnostic labs worldwide.

Core Healthcare Solutions We Deliver

  • Hospital, Doctor & Patient Management Systems: End-to-end digital platforms for appointment scheduling, records, real-time communication, and care workflows.
  • KPI Dashboards & Operational Insights: Live analytics and smart dashboards to monitor clinical KPIs, doctor performance, and departmental efficiency.
  • Behavioral Health & Cognitive Monitoring: Advanced tracking of cognitive patterns, including AI-assisted behavioral health assessments.
  • AI-Powered Diagnostic Systems: We’ve pioneered the use of AI in clinical diagnostics by integrating real-time eye pupil tracking at 240 FPS to identify early signs of conditions like ADHD, Alzheimer’s Disease, and Neurological Impairments — all processed via a secure, cloud-native platform for real-time diagnosis and reporting.

Healthcare Standards & Compliance

  • HL7 Integration: Seamless interoperability between healthcare systems and EHR platforms.
  • EHR/EMR Implementation: Electronic Health Records that are secure, structured, and clinician-friendly.
  • HIPAA & Global Compliance: Built-in data privacy, consent workflows, and secure patient record handling.

Why Choose Anvizor for Healthcare Software?

  • Long years of Proven Experience: Trusted by top providers for reliable healthcare engineering.
  • AI-Driven Innovation: Transforming traditional healthcare with intelligent and data-driven solutions.
  • Full Lifecycle Delivery: From discovery workshops to UAT, deployment, hypercare, and continuous enhancement.
  • Integrated Clinical & Technical Knowledge: Cross-functional teams who understand both patient care and backend systems.
